House Rules

RandomText is designed for respectful, anonymous conversations. By entering a chat room you agree to:

  1. Keep conversations safe-for-school. Absolutely no bullying, harassment, or hateful language.
  2. Never share personal information (names, IDs, phone numbers, addresses, passwords, etc.).
  3. Report any conversation that feels unsafe or breaks these rules.
  4. Remember that conversations may be logged for moderation and learning analytics.
  5. Be kind. If you don’t vibe with someone, hit “Next” and you’ll be matched again.

Questions about the project? Reach out to the BSIT dev team.
